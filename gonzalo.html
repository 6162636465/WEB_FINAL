I am assistant professor at the Department of Computer Science and Director of the Research and Innovation Center in Computer Science (RICS) at San Pablo Catholic University (UCSP), Arequipa/Peru, where also I served as Director of the Undergraduate Program in Computer Science (2018-2019). Before that, from 2017 to 2019 I was Postdoctoral Research Scientist at the Center for the Study of Violence (NEV) at University of São Paulo (USP). From April (2016) to January (2017) I worked as a researcher intern in the Visual Analytics & Comprehension Research Group at IBM Research - BRL. I hold PhD (2017) and MSc (2012) degrees in Computer Science from the Institute of Mathematics and Computer Science (ICMC) - (USP), São Carlos/SP, Brazil. I obtained a B.Sc degree in Informatics Engineering from UCSP. My research interests are in data visualization, visual analytics, machine learning and data science.
